About Ruthless Rocks

Ruthless Rocks opened its doors in 2011, and has been a main source of entertainment for the city since. We offer a 14 foot tall wall that is wide enough to have races with your friends and family to see who can ring the bell at the top first.

While Ruthless Rocks takes the utmost level of care to ensure safety, there is inherent risk and you will be asked to sign a waiver to climb.

We hope to see you here soon!
